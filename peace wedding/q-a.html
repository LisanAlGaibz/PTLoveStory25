Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Formal or traditional attire is encouraged. Please dress to celebrate with us!

Can I bring a plus one?

We kindly ask that only those listed on your invitation attend, due to limited space.

Are children welcome?

While we love our little ones, we’d appreciate you having to seat at the back to avoid kids from interrupting the ceremony and reception.

Will transportation be provided?

Guests are responsible for their own transportation. Please see the Travel & Stay section for recommendations.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 10 AM. Please arrive 15-30 minutes early to be seated.
